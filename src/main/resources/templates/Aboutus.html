Who We Are

BiTES of ADDiS is more than just a platform; it's a vibrant community where culinary passion meets local flavor. We are dedicated to helping you discover the best restaurants, cafes, and eateries, and to providing a stage for local businesses to shine.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a dynamic food ecosystem in Addis Ababa, making it easier for users to find their next favorite meal and for restaurants to connect with their audience. We aim to enhance the dining experience through seamless discovery and engagement.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every food craving can be satisfied with ease, and every hidden culinary gem can be discovered. We strive to be the go-to platform for food exploration, community building, and restaurant success.

Our Core Values

Community First

We believe in empowering local businesses and connecting people through shared food experiences.

Discovery & Innovation

Constantly seeking new ways to help users discover unique places and enhance our platform's features.

Transparency

Providing honest reviews and clear information to help users make informed decisions.
